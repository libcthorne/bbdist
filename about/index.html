About

At BabelBond, we’re not just translating words—we’re building connections that bridge industries, cultures, and borders. In finance and legal services, every word matters, and our mission is to help you communicate with clarity and confidence. We ensure your message resonates as powerfully in New York as it does in Tokyo or Paris.

A Bold Vision,
A Shared Passion

BabelBond was born from a vision shared by two professionals who understand the stakes in global communication. Laura May, a finance expert with a love for languages, and Christopher Thorne, a technology innovator fascinated by bridging cultures, both saw firsthand how the right words could build relationships and open doors. Yet, they also saw the barriers—the struggle to find partners who understood their industries with the same passion and precision.

Refusing to settle, they took a leap to build something new. With BabelBond, Laura and Christopher set out to create a company that would go beyond translations. They envisioned a partner for clients, one who would understand the unique demands of finance and law, and deliver solutions that are precise, culturally attuned, and always aligned with high-stakes needs.

Meet the Founders

With a decade in finance across five countries, Laura May knows the challenges of a global business. Trilingual and well-versed in high-stakes environments, she has supported clients as they embraced digital shifts and navigated complex markets. Her experience in sales & trading, capital markets, wealth management consulting, and investor relations for a global team has shown her exactly what’s needed for international growth. Laura brings that insider knowledge to BabelBond, ensuring that each translation meets the demands of the financial world.

Christopher Thorne’s journey in technology began 15 years ago with a passion for building bridges through language. His career, spanning Japan and the UK, led him to guide multinational companies in making their content accessible worldwide. A former CTO and trilingual problem solver, Christopher turns complex tech into seamless, culturally relevant solutions. At BabelBond, he’s driven to make sure clients’ messages reach every corner of the world with precision, compliance, and cultural resonance.

The BabelBond Promise

At BabelBond, language is a powerful tool to deepen relationships and expand horizons. With the highest standards of care, expertise, and precision, we handle each project as if it were our own, safeguarding your reputation and enhancing your voice across borders.

  • For Finance Leaders: We understand the non-negotiable standards of regulatory compliance. Each report, disclosure, and communication is crafted with accuracy and speed.
